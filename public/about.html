About ConnectSpace

Revolutionizing commercial real estate across India

Our Mission

ConnectSpace is dedicated to creating a transparent, broker-free platform that directly connects commercial property landlords with tenants across India. We believe in eliminating unnecessary intermediaries and building trust through verified listings and community-driven reviews.

Why ConnectSpace?

No Broker Fees

Connect directly with property owners and save on broker commissions. More money in your pocket, better deals for everyone.

Verified Properties

Every property is manually verified through document checks. Our team ensures all listings are legitimate and accurate.

Community Trust

Our review system allows tenants to rate landlords and properties, creating a transparent marketplace built on trust.

Local Focus

Specifically designed for the Indian commercial real estate market, understanding local needs and regulations.

How It Works

1

List Your Property

Landlords create detailed listings with photos and upload required documents for verification.

2

Verification Process

Our team manually reviews all documents within 24 hours and awards verified badges to legitimate properties.

3

Direct Connection

Tenants search, find, and directly contact landlords through our secure messaging system.

4

Build Trust

After successful transactions, both parties can leave reviews to help future users make informed decisions.

Our Commitment

We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of transparency and trust. Every property listing is verified, every user interaction is secure, and every transaction is facilitated with complete honesty. Our goal is to make commercial real estate transactions as simple and trustworthy as possible.
