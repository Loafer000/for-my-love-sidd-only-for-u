Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ConnectSpace

How do I list my property?

Click on "List Your Property" and fill out the detailed form. Upload required documents for verification. Your property will be live within 24 hours after verification.

Is there any fee for listing?

Basic listings are completely free. We plan to introduce premium features in the future for enhanced visibility and analytics.

How do you verify properties?

Our team manually reviews all uploaded documents including Sale Deed, Property Tax Receipt, and other relevant papers to ensure legitimacy.

Can I edit my listing?

Yes, you can edit your listings anytime through the Landlord Dashboard. Changes will be reflected immediately.

How long does verification take?

Our verification process typically takes 24-48 hours. You'll receive an email notification once your property is verified and live.

How do I connect with tenants?

Once your property is listed, interested tenants can contact you directly through our secure messaging system or phone.
